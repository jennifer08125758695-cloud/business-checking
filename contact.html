Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept guest posts or contributed articles?

Yes! We welcome contributions from personal finance experts, certified financial planners, and experienced writers. Please email your pitch to editorial@smartmoneydaily.com with the subject line "Guest Post Pitch."

How do I advertise with Smart Money Daily?

We offer sponsored content placements, newsletter sponsorships, and display advertising. Please contact advertise@smartmoneydaily.com for our media kit and rates.

How do I unsubscribe from your newsletter?

Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link at the bottom. Simply click it to be removed from our mailing list immediately. You can also email us and we'll remove you manually within 24 hours.

I found an error in an article. How do I report it?

We take accuracy seriously. Please use the contact form above and select "Request a Correction." Include the article title, the error you found, and the correct information if you know it. We'll review and update the article promptly.
